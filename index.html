Description

 

History

We started out with an idea of an arcade game that revolves around the limit of the protagonist's imagination. After researching time and then fine tuning our concept, we took inspiration from Warioware and aim to create a game with surrealism as its foundation. That things can go weird because they can. The final concept is a mix of both of the original, with a protagonist at the middle of all the wacky things unfold.

Features

  • Various types of minigames.
  • Reference to different genres/playstyles.
  • Linear yet confusing story.
  • Cartoonish graphics.
